The background-clip Property

The background-clip property defines how far the background should extend within an element.

Text with image as background, -webkit-background-clip: text (background-clip: text doesn't work):

The background extends to the edge of the content box.

background-clip: border-box (this is default):

The background extends behind the border.

background-clip: padding-box:

The background extends to the inside edge of the border.

background-clip: content-box:

The background extends to the edge of the content box.

[-webkit-background-clip: border-box] without simple [background-clip], and background-color instead of background:

The background extends to the edge of the content box.
